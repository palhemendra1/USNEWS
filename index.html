Affordability Reimagined

A comprehensive analysis of 25 U.S. cities using expanded economic metrics to identify true livability.

📊 What We Did

We analyzed 25 U.S. cities using a proprietary affordability score combining five core factors: median income, rent, home value, cost of living rank, and local unemployment rates.

💡 Why It Matters

Unlike traditional indices, we include unemployment — a key indicator of economic stress. This provides a holistic view of where Americans can find both affordability and stability.

Conducted by independent researchers using publicly available data, intended to support inclusive reporting on the real-world housing crisis in America.
